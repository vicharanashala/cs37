Frequently Asked Questions

Admissions

What are the eligibility criteria for the internship program?

Applicants must be currently enrolled in a recognised undergraduate or postgraduate programme. A minimum of 60% aggregate in the most recent academic year is required. The programme is open to students from all disciplines, though preference is given to candidates with a background in Computer Science, Information Technology, or related fields.

How do I apply for the internship?

Applications are submitted through the Samagama student portal. Navigate to the 'Internships' section under the 'Careers' tab. Complete the application form, upload your resume, and submit a brief statement of purpose. Incomplete applications are not considered.

When is the application deadline?

The internship programme runs on a rolling cycle with four intakes per year: January, April, July, and October. Applications must be submitted at least 30 days before the start of the preferred intake. Exact dates are published on the portal at least 60 days in advance.

Offer Letter & Onboarding

When do I get the offer letter?

Your offer letter is issued within 24–48 hours after NOC verification. It is sent to your registered email address. If you do not receive it within 72 hours, please contact the HR team through the student portal.

What documents are required for onboarding?

You need to submit: (1) Signed offer letter copy, (2) Government-issued photo ID (Aadhaar, PAN, or Passport), (3) Latest academic transcripts, (4) NOC from your institution, (5) Passport-size photographs (2 copies). All documents must be uploaded as PDFs through the portal.

Stipend & Benefits

What is the stipend amount?

The stipend varies by role and duration. The standard range is ₹10,000 – ₹25,000 per month for full-time internships. Part-time interns receive a pro-rated amount. Exact figures are mentioned in the offer letter. Stipends are disbursed by the 7th of every month.

Are there any additional benefits?

Yes. Interns receive: (1) Certificate of completion, (2) Letter of recommendation upon satisfactory performance, (3) Access to select online courses on the Samagama learning platform, (4) Mentorship from senior team members, and (5) Networking opportunities with industry professionals.

Policies & Code of Conduct

What is the leave policy for interns?

Interns are entitled to 1 day of leave per month, pro-rated for shorter durations. Leaves must be approved by your reporting manager at least 48 hours in advance. Medical emergencies are handled on a case-by-case basis with appropriate documentation.

What is the code of conduct?

All interns must adhere to the Samagama Code of Conduct, which includes maintaining confidentiality of proprietary information, respecting workplace diversity, adhering to deadlines, and refraining from any form of harassment. Violations may result in termination of the internship.
