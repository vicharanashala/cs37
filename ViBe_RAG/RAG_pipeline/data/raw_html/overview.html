Samagama Internship Programme Overview

About the Programme

The Samagama Internship Programme is designed to provide students and recent graduates with hands-on experience in real-world projects. Interns work alongside experienced professionals, contributing to meaningful initiatives while developing industry-relevant skills. The programme emphasises mentorship, continuous learning, and professional growth.

Our mission is to bridge the gap between academic knowledge and industry practice, equipping participants with the tools they need to succeed in their careers.

Programme Structure

The internship is structured into three phases:

  • Orientation Week: An introduction to Samagama's culture, tools, technologies, and expectations. Interns are assigned mentors and given an overview of their projects.
  • Core Work Phase: Interns work on assigned projects under the guidance of their mentors. This phase includes weekly check-ins, skill-building workshops, and mid-point evaluations.
  • Completion & Review: Interns submit a final report, deliver a presentation on their work, and receive a comprehensive performance review. Certificates and recommendation letters are issued upon successful completion.

Available Roles

We offer internships across multiple domains:

  • Software Development: Full-stack web development using the MERN stack (MongoDB, Express, React, Node.js).
  • AI/ML Engineering: Building and deploying machine learning models, working on RAG pipelines, and natural language processing systems.
  • Data Science: Data analysis, visualisation, and insight generation using Python and related tools.
  • UI/UX Design: Designing user interfaces and experiences for web and mobile applications using Figma and modern design principles.
  • Technical Writing: Creating documentation, guides, and technical content for internal and external audiences.

Expectations & Commitment

Interns are expected to commit a minimum of 20 hours per week for part-time roles and 40 hours per week for full-time roles. All interns must have a reliable internet connection and a personal laptop. Remote work is supported, but occasional in-person meetings may be scheduled depending on the role and location.

Professional communication, timely task completion, and active participation in team activities are mandatory. Interns are encouraged to ask questions, seek feedback, and take initiative in their work.
